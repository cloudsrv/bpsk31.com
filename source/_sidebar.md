###### PSK31 Frequencies

1.838<br>
3.580<br>

10.140<br>
14.070<br>
18.100<br>
21.080 (although most activity can be found 10 kHz lower)<br>

\* The 7 MHz band is much wider in region 2 (the Americas), thus 7.080 is added for that region.  7.000 to 7.040 is CW only in region 1.
